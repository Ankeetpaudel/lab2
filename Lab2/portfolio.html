Exploring the Excellence of UIDAHO

The University of Idaho, founded in 1889, is a prestigious institution known for its commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and fostering an inclusive community. Explore the unique qualities that make UIDAHO a standout choice for higher education.

UIDAHO offers a diverse range of academic programs, spanning engineering, agriculture, business, humanities, and sciences. Our faculty members are dedicated to providing students with a comprehensive education that prepares them for success in their chosen fields.

At UIDAHO, we take pride in our commitment to research excellence. Our faculty and students engage in cutting-edge research projects that contribute to advancements in various disciplines. Explore the exciting world of research at the University of Idaho.

Experience a vibrant and inclusive campus life at UIDAHO. From modern facilities and recreational spaces to a diverse student body, our campus provides a welcoming environment for personal and academic growth. Discover the various extracurricular activities that enrich the student experience.

UIDAHO encourages students to actively participate in community engagement and service projects. Explore how our students make a positive impact locally and globally through their involvement in various community initiatives.

Connect with us to embark on a transformative academic journey at the University of Idaho. Follow us on social media for updates and insights into the UIDAHO experience.
